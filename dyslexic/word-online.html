How to use Word Online

  1. Go to this website and sign in to your school/Microsoft account using the button on the top right.
  2. Once signed in, you can create a blank document or upload a file. Since in Word Online they force you to start page numbers at 0 instead of 1, (which is against MLA formatting,) I highly recommend you use my MLA document template which you can download by clicking this link.
  3. If you are using my template, upload it and replace the text with your own, and rename it to not conflict filenames if you use it multiple times. If you'd prefer to format the document yourself, however, you can find my tutorial on how to do exactly that here.
